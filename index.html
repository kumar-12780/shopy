Our Story

"Every candle is a conversation between fragrance and memory."

Lumo began in a small studio in Coorg — a labour of love, wax, and afternoon light. We believe that fragrance is more than scent; it is an anchor to feeling, to place, to self.

Each candle is hand-poured in batches of twenty, using 100% natural soy wax and botanically-sourced fragrance oils. No shortcuts. No rush. Just craft, done slowly.

— Priya, Founder

Handmade with Intention

Poured in batches of twenty, each candle is crafted by hand in our Coorg studio — never rushed, never mass-produced.

100% Natural Ingredients

Soy wax. Cotton wicks. Phthalate-free fragrance oils. Clean ingredients, nothing synthetic, nothing that doesn't belong.

Long-Lasting Fragrance

Each candle carries 40–55 hours of fragrance — slow-burning, true-to-scent, and designed to fill a room beautifully.
